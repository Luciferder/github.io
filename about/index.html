Short Bio

I'm currently a second year master's student at Data Mining Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). Prior to that, I got my B.Eng. Degree in Yingcai Honors College, UESTC in 2016. I am supervised by Prof. Junming Shao, who has been advising me since my junior year in university.

My research interests lay in Data Mining and Machine Learning, with a focus on leveraging graph analysis and clustering techniques, to a wide range of questions including Spatio-temporal data analysis, pattern-based graph embedding, recommender system and so on.
