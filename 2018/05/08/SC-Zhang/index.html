Correlation Clustering via Low-Rank Segmentation Model

Zhong Zhang, Chongming Gao, Qinli Yang, Junming Shao

In this paper, towards the correlation clustering problem, we propose an effective algorithm to find arbitrarily oriented correlation clusters via low-rank segmentation model. An orthonormal rigid transformation to explicitly obtain subspace for each cluster. In addition, Our model integrates a strategy to enhance the robustness of finding correlation clusters. Besides, Our model can naturally identify the noises as well as the overlapping points during the optimization process. The extensive experiments on synthetic and real-world datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm.
